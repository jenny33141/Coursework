Am I happy with my Website?


2.3 Assess the strengths and weaknesses of your final project - are you happy with your website? Why? Why not? You should review it on the website in the interests of openness. What would you change?


Given the time constraints and steep learning curve, I am extremely happy with my website. It functions, it is clear and it is easy to navigate.


There are, however, many things that I would change if I was given the same project again. I would like to give it more styling and a more contemporary feel. I feel it looks too basic and dated.

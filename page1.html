The Purpose Of IT In Modern Business


1.1 What is the purpose of I.T in modern business?


I.T. is used in many different ways in modern business its predominant use is to increase business productivity. It vastly improves communication with customers and suppliers; this, in turn, can help to increase loyalty and better working relationships. Technology makes tracking sales effortless making it much easier to control stock and cash flow.


Providing the ability to store large amounts of data, it can help businesses easily keep track of customer records and, in some cases, better understand customer needs. Websites and social media can provide a business with a much larger geographical reach. Using software security, businesses can easily protect financial and personal details, as well as business ideas and concepts from any outside threat or competitor.

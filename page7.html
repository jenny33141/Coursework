Legal Guidelines


1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects - How does GDPR impact digital projects?


All digital projects have to comply with GDPR. This means developers have to understand all privacy rules and implement them. These are to do with the storage and processing of personal data. All GDPR rules must be followed and strictly adhered to. The rules must be considered in all stages of development from planning to testing and must still be prominent when undertaking any updates or maintenance.

Copyright




1.5 describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites.


Copyright is legal protection for literary, dramatic, musical, artistic, film or broadcast work. It means the rights for use belong to the creator of the work. Great care needs to be taken not to infringe these laws when building a website. All consumer laws must also be understood these cover data protection, e-commerce, accessibility amongst others.
